# Key References & Seminal Papers

### Academic Papers
*   **Baek, J. S., Kang, J. K., & Park, K. S. (2004).** *Corporate Governance and Firm Value: Evidence from the Korean Financial Crisis.* Journal of Financial Economics.
*   **Eberhart, R. N. (2012).** *Corporate Governance Systems and Firm Value: Empirical Evidence from Japan's Natural Experiment.* Stanford University.
*   **Miyajima, H., & Saito, T. (2023).** *The Impact of Corporate Governance Reforms on Cross-shareholdings in Japan.* RIETI Discussion Paper.
*   **Bereskin, G., Kim, H., & Oh, J. (2015).** *Do Credit Rating Concerns Lead to Better Corporate Governance? Evidence from Korea.* Financial Management.

### Institutional Reports
*   **KCMI (2023).** *Analysis on the Causes behind the Korea Discount.* (Kim Joon-seok et al.). The definitive report on the three pillars of the discount.
*   **IMF (2021).** *The Impact of Geopolitical Risk on Stock Returns: Evidence from Inter-Korea Geopolitics.* (Working Paper 21/251). Introduces the GPRNK Index.
*   **Goldman Sachs (2022).** *Korea: What if? Strong upside from potential MSCI DM reclassification.*
*   **KCMI (2024).** *Market Accessibility of Korean Capital Markets: Viewpoint of Foreign Institutions.*

### Recent Legislative/Policy Context
*   **Korea "Value-Up" Program (2024-2025):** Guidelines and updates from the Financial Services Commission (FSC).
*   **Commercial Act Amendments (2025):** Expansion of fiduciary duties to include "shareholders."
